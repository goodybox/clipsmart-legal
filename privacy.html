Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 16, 2025

Overview

ClipSmart is a clipboard manager for macOS that helps you organize and manage your clipboard history with AI-powered features. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

1. Clipboard Data

  • ClipSmart stores your clipboard history locally on your device only
  • This data never leaves your device except when you explicitly use AI features
  • You have full control and can delete your clipboard history at any time
  • We do not have access to your clipboard data

2. AI Features

  • When you use AI features (Classify, Summarize, Translate), the specific content is sent to OpenAI's API
  • We do not store, log, or retain this data
  • The data is processed by OpenAI according to their privacy policy: https://openai.com/privacy
  • AI features are optional - you can use ClipSmart without them

3. Usage Statistics

  • We track feature usage counts locally to enforce subscription limits
  • This data is stored only on your device
  • No personally identifiable information is collected
  • No analytics or tracking services are used

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your Mac using SQLite database

  • No cloud storage or syncing
  • Data remains on your device unless you delete the app
  • We have no access to your data

Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI API: Used for AI features when you explicitly request them
  • Apple App Store: Manages subscription payments and renewals

Your Rights

  • Delete all clipboard data at any time via the "Clear All" button
  • Cancel your subscription at any time through App Store settings
  • Request information about your data by contacting us

Data Security

  • Your clipboard data is stored locally using macOS security features
  • AI API requests are sent over HTTPS encryption
  • We recommend using FileVault for additional device encryption

Children's Privacy

ClipSmart is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use of ClipSmart after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
